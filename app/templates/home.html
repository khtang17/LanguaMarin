Welcome to German Language 101, a German Language School in San Rafael, Marin County, California!

We offer German language classes for children and adults at all levels. We always have small classes so that we can give each student the personal attention he or she deserves. German Language 101 is different from other German language schools, because we tailor the course to suit the students. We select authentic German books and multimedia to match the level, interests, and objectives of the pupils.

Learning German at any age can be beneficial, but it is particularly advantageous for children. We have a teaching philosophy that focuses on learning by speaking. You can read more about who we are here.
