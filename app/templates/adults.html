Adult Students

Group classes have a maximum of six students each, so that each student receives personal attention. Classes start in September (fall term) and February (spring term). Classes do start at other times corresponding to the needs of our students. Students can join classes at any time of the year and tuition is pro-rated.

We offer a free consultation and evaluation lesson to every prospective student. The purpose of this meeting is to find out what level and kind of lessons are right for the student. An added benefit is that the student can meet the teacher and find out more about us.
