Adult Students

Group classes have a maximum of six students each, so that each student receives personal attention. Classes start in September (fall term) and February (spring term). Classes do start at other times corresponding to the needs of our students. Students can join classes at any time of the year and tuition is pro-rated.

We offer a free consultation and evaluation lesson to every prospective student. The purpose of this meeting is to find out what level and kind of lessons are right for the student. An added benefit is that the student can meet the teacher and find out more about us.

Classes are available for various levels and ages

    • Level: Beginners Level A1.1(complete beginners)
    • Level: Beginners Level A2.1
    • Level: Beginners Level A2.1
  • Intermediate German
    • Level: Intermediate Level B1.1
    • Level: Intermediate Level B1.2
    • Level: German Culture Through Film (Movie Nights)
  • Advanced German
    • Level B2
    • German Culture Through Film (Movie Nights)
  • German for Business
    • For the convenience of our business customers, we can offer classes at your place of work in Mill Valley, Corte Madera, San Anselmo, Fairfax, Greenbrae, Kentfield, Larkspur, Novato, Ross, Sausalito, Tiburon, San Rafael. We can also offer classes in Sonoma and Napa.
  • Private and Semi-Private Instruction
    • If your schedule does not permit attending our scheduled classes, you may be interested in private instruction. We can arrange classes at times and locations to suit your busy schedule, at a price that offers excellent value for money. Please contact us for details.

For our clients who travel, move to or live in a German-speaking country, we offer German Anywhere®, a new and convenient way to learn German at a time and place that suits you.

Here is how it works.

  • You purchase the textbook for the course.
  • You connect to the internet using your computer.
  • Before the lesson begins, you will be sent any additional materials necessary.
  • You follow lessons with two way audio and video and other multimedia directly between you and your German Language 101 teacher.

Depending on what time zone you are in, lesson times can be chosen that are convenient for you. There is no additional fee for this service beyond the normal German Language 101 rate.

Do you think this might work for you? Trial lessons at German Language 101 are always free and come with no obligation. So why not contact us today to see whether our German Anywhere® service is right for you?
